Let me explain

I can describe my work experience as an exciting journey through different design related fields. From architectural visualisation and representation to taking part in making educational digital media solutions, from user interface creation for mobile games, to providing promo materials and concepts for stage performances and shows.

That versatility is a result of curiosity and passion for new challenges. It always good to learn something new, it could be the technology or creative aproach, and perhaps the most effective way to learn is to work with talented people. I concider myself as a multidisciplinary designer and trying to evolve both creatively and technically.
