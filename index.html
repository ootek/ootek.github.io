Let me explain

A various pieces of concept art and UI design are presented here as a result of four years working period. The goal was to make a consistent-looking presentation with 'roll your own' design approach, and also, practice a bit with HTML/CSS layouts.

I am interested in both aesthetic and technical aspects of UI design as well as typography and digital illustration. As a production artist I’m enjoying the opportunity to create visual languages by making environmental or stage design concepts.
